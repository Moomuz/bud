About the CEO

As a child, I enjoyed gardening with my father especially during the summer. But, now that I have become an adult and moved to the city years later, I can no longer enjoy that same passion the same way that I used to. However, that didn't stop me from having my own graden indoors!


For many people like me in the city, they aren't able to have full-blown gardens as easily as people who live anywhere else. That's why I decided to create simple DIY plant kits that is easy for everyone to use. Here at Bud Co., not only do we strive to make gardening an available activity for everyone, but we also want to promote the protection of the environment.


— Grace McGraw, CEO of Bud Co.
