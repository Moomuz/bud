To start a new bud, open a new plant kic. Work in a preferrably open area to avoid any mess and take out the bud package's contents. Seeds, dirt, pot, and plant food are already included so you don't have to worry about them! All you need is water.

Follow the packages instructions on how to plant your bud. Each plant is different so treat them nicely! However, all of our buds follow the typical dig a hole, plant the seeds, and cover it up process. Don't worry because plenty of seeds are provided.

Then just water! Easy as that. Make sure to water according to package instructions to keep your bud healthy. Now just set your bud anywhere you'd like, preferrably in a place where it'll get plenty of sunshine. Happy planting!
