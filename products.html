Succulent Series

Echeveria

$6.95

In general, succulents are desert plants that are so easy to take of because of their desert-dry origins. The echeveria is one of the most commonly-known because of its beaustiful rose-like shape.

Sedum

$6.95

Sedums are like the mini version of the above echeveria plant that come in little bunches, though its leaf shape is much rounder and skinnier. Many gardeners find that these look better over a large area, but they are just as cute in a small clay pot as well.

Graptopetalum

$6.95

Though its long long name may be difficult to pronounce, it isn't hard to see that graptopetalums are a beautiful type of succulents. But don't get this one confused with echeverias because graptopetalums have thicker and pointier leaves. So choose wisely!
